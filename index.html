What is Tatts Lotto?

Tatts Lotto is one of Australia’s most popular national lotteries, originally launched in Victoria in 1972. It's known under different names across states: Saturday Lotto, Gold Lotto, and X Lotto, but it's the same thrilling game of chance loved nationwide.

With a standard Division 1 prize pool of around $5 million weekly, Tatts Lotto also hosts exciting special draws like the $20 Million Superdraw and the massive Megadraw events, giving players even more chances to win life-changing prizes.

How Does Tatts Lotto Work?

Each Saturday, Tatts Lotto draws 8 numbers from a barrel containing 45 balls numbered 1 to 45. The first 6 balls drawn are your main winning numbers, while the final 2 are called supplementary numbers. These bonus numbers help determine winners in Divisions 2 and 5, offering more chances to score a prize.

Common Questions and Answers

What is the policy for refunding lottery tickets?

Lottery tickets are non-refundable. If there are any issues with your ticket purchase or payment, please reach out to our customer support team, and we will assist you accordingly.

How can I track my lottery ticket purchase?

You can track your lottery ticket purchase by logging into your account on our website. We will also send you an email confirmation with your ticket details after purchase.

Do you ship tickets internationally?

Currently, we only provide digital tickets, so international shipping is not required. You can play from anywhere in the world as long as you meet the age requirement and are in an eligible region.

How can I claim my winnings?

If you win a prize, you will be notified via email. To claim your winnings, you may need to provide identification and follow the instructions sent to you.

Can I play the lottery on my mobile device?

Yes, our website is fully mobile-responsive, and you can easily play the lottery using your smartphone or tablet from anywhere.

Is there an age limit to participate in the lottery?

Yes, participants must be at least 18 years old to play the lottery. Please make sure you comply with the legal age requirements in your country.

How are the lottery draws performed?

All lottery draws are conducted using a fair and transparent random number generator (RNG) system to ensure a fair process. You can view the results live or on our website after each draw.

Can I purchase multiple tickets for one draw?

Yes, you can buy as many tickets as you like for each draw. The more tickets you purchase, the higher your chances of winning.
