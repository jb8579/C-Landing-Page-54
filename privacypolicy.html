Your Privacy Matters

At Tatts Lotto (techdefault.com), your privacy is our priority.

We understand how important your privacy is and are committed to ensuring that any personal information you share with us is handled with care and transparency. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, disclose, and protect your information when you interact with our services.

Information We Collect:

To provide a personalized and secure experience, we collect various types of information depending on how you use our site.

  • Personal information such as your full name, email address, phone number, and payment data when you sign up, purchase tickets, or claim winnings.

  • Device, browser, IP address, and behavioral data via cookies and similar technologies to enhance your user experience.

How We Use Your Information:

The information we collect enables us to run our services smoothly and communicate effectively with you. Here’s how we use your data:

  • Facilitate lottery ticket purchases and validate winning claims.

  • Deliver a smooth and secure experience across our platform.

  • Send you updates, promotions, and service-related notifications.

  • Fulfill our obligations under applicable laws and regulations.

Information Sharing:

We do not sell your personal information. However, there are specific cases in which we may share it responsibly:

  • Trusted third-party vendors who help us with operations such as payment processing, analytics, or marketing.

  • Government bodies or regulators if required for compliance or legal purposes.

  • Relevant parties in the event of a business transfer such as a merger, acquisition, or asset sale.

Data Security:

We take data security seriously and apply a range of security measures to protect your information:

  • We use industry-standard encryption, secure server configurations, and access controls to keep your information safe.

Your Rights:

We believe in transparency and give you full control over your data. You have the right to:

  • Request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal data at any time.

  • Opt out of marketing emails by using the unsubscribe link in our messages.

Changes to this Policy:

Our Privacy Policy may be updated occasionally to reflect changes in legal, regulatory, or operational needs.

  • We reserve the right to amend this Privacy Policy as our services evolve.

  • Significant changes will be communicated via email or prominently posted on our website.

Contact Us:

If you have any questions, feedback, or concerns regarding this Privacy Policy or how we manage your data, feel free to contact our support team:

  • Email: info@techdefault.com
